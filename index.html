ARC Leeds Training Materials

This is an archive of in-house training materials developed by the Advanced Research Computing Team at the University of Leeds. For an easy-to browse overview of course material, please visit our website.

For some of our courses, we may use external public materials which are linked separately below. Our materials are designed to work both as a basis for synchronous training sessions, as as documentation for self-directed study. This also facilitates flipped-learning, allowing you to prepare before a session.

This will be updated as our courses evolve and change. Please check our website to book training.
